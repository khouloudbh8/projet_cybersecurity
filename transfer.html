ğŸ›¡ï¸ Security Best Practices for Money Transfers

Before You Send:

  • Verify the recipient's identity through multiple channels
  • Double-check account numbers and routing information
  • Ensure you're on the official website (check URL and HTTPS)
  • Never transfer money based on unsolicited requests

During the Transfer:

  • Use a secure internet connection (avoid public Wi-Fi)
  • Never share your password or 2FA codes
  • Review all details carefully before confirming
  • Save confirmation numbers and transaction IDs

After Sending:

  • Monitor your account for the deduction
  • Keep records of all transfer documentation
  • Verify the recipient received the funds
  • Report any discrepancies immediately
